About HealthBridge

We connect community members to safely share in-date medicines, reduce waste, and improve access to care.

About HealthBridge

HealthBridge is a community-driven platform connecting people who need medicines with generous donors. Our mission is to reduce medicine waste while improving access to essential medications for those in need.
