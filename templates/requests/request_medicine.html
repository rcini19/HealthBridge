Provide Clear Medical Reasoning
Explain your medical condition and why you need this medicine
Good Examples:
• "I have been diagnosed with Type 2 Diabetes and need this medication for blood sugar control as prescribed by my doctor"
• "Suffering from bacterial throat infection. Doctor prescribed this antibiotic for 7-day treatment"
• "Chronic hypertension patient, need this medicine for daily blood pressure management"
Avoid Vague Statements:
• "I need it" (not specific enough)
• "For pain" (what condition? what diagnosis?)
• "Just because" (no medical reasoning)
